Lion

The lion is known as the king of the jungle. It is a large carnivorous feline with a majestic mane.

Tiger

The tiger is the largest species among the Felidae and is recognizable for its pattern of dark vertical stripes on reddish-orange fur.

Elephant

Elephants are the largest land animals on Earth, known for their intelligence, strong social bonds, and long trunks.

Giraffe

The giraffe is the tallest living terrestrial animal, famous for its long neck and legs.

Zebra

Zebras are known for their distinctive black and white striped coats, which help them camouflage in the wild.

Monkey

Monkeys are intelligent primates known for their agility and playful behavior.
